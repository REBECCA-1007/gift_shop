Our Story

Founded by Rebecca, Rebecca’s Choice started from a simple joy: tiny, beautiful objects that make big moments warmer. We curate and craft mini gifts with a boutique approach — thoughtful materials, elegant colors, and packaging that feels special to open.

From weddings and birthdays to seasonal holidays, we believe the best gifts are the ones that carry a memory. Every set is made gift-ready with care, so you can celebrate without the hassle.

Hand-assembled in small batches. Limited drops each season.

Craft & Process

Each mini set is assembled by hand in our studio: we test scents, pair textures, and refine unboxing down to the last ribbon. Color stories are tuned to the occasion — pearl whites & blush for weddings, cozy reds & greens for Christmas, fresh tones for summer.

Packaging is recyclable where possible. We minimize excess and keep things reusable.
